Tableau d'informations sur l'entreprise

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German luxury automobile manufacturer founded in 1916. It is one of the best-known and most respected car brands in the world, known for producing high-performance and stylish vehicles. BMWs are known for their precise handling and strong engines, and the company has a reputation for building premium cars with a level of refinement and attention to detail that is second to none. Some of the most popular models in the BMW lineup include the 3 Series, 5 Series, 7 Series, and X3. BMW also produces a range of electric and hybrid vehicles, including the i3 and i8.
